Ingredients:

200 g lentils
1 large onion, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
2 celery stalks, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
400 g diced tomatoes
1.5 liters vegetable broth
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp paprika
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tbsp olive oil

Add garlic, cumin, and paprika.
